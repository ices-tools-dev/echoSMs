# Training resources

_This section is under development_

Choosing and using acoustic scattering models can be difficult. to help, this page provides links
 to some helpful resources and presentations. Suggestions
of additional items for the list below are welcome, as are other training materials that may
be relevant.

- An [overview](https://github.com/SvenGastauer/scatmod) of scattering models types and uses
- The echoSMs [presentation](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/blob/main/docs/resources/2025%20WGFAST%20presentation/WGFAST%20echoSMs%20presentation%20V2.pdf) to the 2025 meeting of the ICES FAST working group
- The [Jupyter notebook](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/blob/main/docs/WGFAST2025_demo.ipynb) used during the 2025 echoSMS WG-FAST presentation
- The [presentation](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/blob/main/docs/resources/2025%20WGFAST%20workshop/WGFAST2025%20echoSMs%20workshop%20presentation.pdf) for the 2025 echoSMs workshop held during the ICES FAST working group
- The [Jupyter notebook](https://github.com/ices-tools-dev/echoSMs/blob/main/docs/Workshop%20demo.ipynb) used during the 2025 echoSMS WG-FAST workshop
